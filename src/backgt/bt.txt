1 Types:
1.1 ?x!?y[?z] :- nonvar(?z)@_prolog,
                 ?x!?y[?t],
                 ?t::?z;
1.2 ?x:?y :- typing(?t),
             ?t = inference,
             ?z!?t[?y],
             ?u:?z,
             ?u.?t[?x];
2 Subtypes:
2.1 ?x::?y :- ?x::?z,
              ?z::?y,
              ?x!=?y.
3 Membership:
3.1 ?x:?y :- ?x:?z,
             ?z::?y;
4 Equality:
4.1 ?x:=:?x;
4.2 ?x:=:?y :- ?y:=:?x;
4.3 ?x:=:?y :- ?x:=:?z,
               ?y:=:?z;
